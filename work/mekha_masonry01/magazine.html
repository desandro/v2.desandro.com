Magazine Example

I've put together this example of what your client is probably looking for. There are three sizes used:

  • .col1 : 220px
  • .col2 : 460px
  • .col3 : 700px
With 10px of margin on right and left sides that makes for a grid of 240px.

Setting up masonry is easy for this example. The only option we need is to specify the column width. Window resizing is set up in the defaults.

$(function(){
	$('#example').masonry({columnWidth: 240})
})

Like I said the hardest part is going to be reducing the gaps. This is hard to do with boxes of varying sizes. After looking at Space Collective gallery, I believe they acheived the effect by specifically placing certain sized boxes in certain columns. When you size it to three columns, all 1-column boxes are in the left column, and 2-column boxes are in the right. When you size it 4 columns wide, its 1 - 2 - 1. Only when you size it to 5 columns does it mix it up.

If I were to try to reproduce this effect, I would need a lot more time and that's not the case for this project. But I don't think having gaps is necessarily a bad thing. I've styled the boxes so there's no outlines. Where there are gaps, it's not that distracting. If anything, it helps separate one section from another.
